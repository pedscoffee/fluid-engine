Dual-Context Conversations

Contextura maintains two separate conversation threads: your main chat and a side panel for questions, analysis, or commentary that won't pollute your primary conversation.

Tip: Use templates to quickly set up different conversation styles!

100% Private & Local

Everything runs in your browser. Your conversations never leave your device. No servers, no tracking, completely private.

First-time setup: The AI model downloads once (~2GB). After that, everything is instant and offline!

Ready to Chat!

You can type or use push-to-talk. The side panel provides context-aware commentary without disrupting your main conversation.
